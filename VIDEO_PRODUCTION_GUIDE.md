### Phase 1: Planning (1-2 days)

#### 1. Define Video Goals
- **Target Audience**: Puzzle game lovers, casual mobile gamers
- **Video Purpose**: App Store preview, YouTube trailer, social media ads
- **Key Messages**: 
  - Relaxing zen experience

**Option A: App Store Preview (15-30 seconds)**
- Quick gameplay showcase
- No voiceover, just music

**Option B: YouTube Trailer (60-90 seconds)**

- Call-to-action at end

**Option C: Social Media Cut (15 seconds)**
- Fast-paced highlights

- Strong CTA

### Phase 2: Recording Gameplay (2-3 hours)

#### Tools Needed
- **Screen Recorder**: 
  - iOS: Built-in iOS screen recorder
  - Android: AZ Screen Recorder, Mobizen
  - Emulator: BlueStacks, NoxPlayer

#### Recording Tips

**Best Levels to Record**:
1. Tutorial level (show basic mechanics)
2. Mid-game level (show ice blocks, boosters)
3. Challenging level (show strategy and power-ups)

**Recording Settings**:
- Resolution: 1080p or higher
- Frame rate: 60fps
- Audio: Record game sounds + music
- Duration: 2-3 minutes per level

**Step-by-Step**:
1. Open Mahjong Heaven game
2. Start screen recording
3. Play through 3-5 different levels
4. Capture special moments:
   - Matching 3 tiles
   - Breaking ice blocks
   - Using power-ups
   - Level completion animation
   - Star rating reveal

### Phase 3: Editing (4-6 hours)

#### Recommended Software

**Free Options**:
- DaVinci Resolve (Professional, free)
- CapCut (Mobile, easy to use)
- iMovie (Mac, simple)
- Shotcut (Open source)

**Paid Options**:
- Adobe Premiere Pro ($22.99/mo)
- Final Cut Pro ($299 one-time)
- Filmora ($49.99/year)

#### Video Structure

**Hook (0-3 seconds)**:
- Eye-catching gameplay
- Bold text: "THE MOST RELAXING PUZZLE GAME"
- Calming music starts

**Introduction (3-10 seconds)**:
- Game logo animation
- Tagline: "Mahjong Heaven: Sort & Merge"
- Platform logos: iOS + Android

**Gameplay Showcase (10-40 seconds)**:
- Show matching mechanics
- Ice block breaking
- Power-up usage
- Level transitions
- Quick cuts between levels

**Features (40-50 seconds)**:
- Text overlays:
  - "200+ Handcrafted Levels"
  - "Stress Relief"
  - "Play Anywhere"
- Feature highlights

**Call-to-Action (50-60 seconds)**:
- Download buttons
- App Store + Google Play logos
- "Download Free Now"
- Fade to black with logo

### Phase 4: Audio Enhancement

#### Music Selection

**Royalty-Free Music Sites**:
- YouTube Audio Library (Free)
- Epidemic Sound ($15/mo)
- Artlist ($16.50/mo)
- Free Music Archive (Free)

**Music Style**:
- Calming, zen-inspired
- Ambient, meditation
- Traditional Asian instruments
- Moderate tempo (60-90 BPM)

**Recommended Tracks**:
- Search: "Zen meditation", "Asian ambient", "Calming puzzle"
- Avoid: Heavy electronic, aggressive beats

#### Sound Effects

**Keep Game Sounds**:
- Tile matching sounds
- Ice breaking
- Power-up activation
- Level completion

**Add Additional Sounds**:
- Soft swoosh for transitions
- Gentle chime for text reveals
- Subtle whoosh for logo animations

### Phase 5: Visual Polish

#### Text Overlays

**Font Recommendations**:
- Montserrat or Poppins (Modern, clean)
- Color: White with black outline
- Size: Large enough for mobile
- Animation: Fade in, slide up

**Key Text**:
- "Match 3 to Clear"
- "200+ Levels"
- "Stress Relief"
- "Download Free"

#### Color Grading

**Style**:
- Slightly boost saturation
- Increase contrast
- Add warm tone (emerald/gold accent)
- Keep tiles vibrant

**Effects**:
- Subtle glow on matches
- Smooth transitions
- Light particle effects

### Phase 6: Export & Optimization

#### Export Settings

**YouTube/Full Version**:
- Resolution: 1920x1080 (1080p)
- Codec: H.264
- Bitrate: 8-10 Mbps
- Frame rate: 30fps or 60fps
- Audio: AAC, 192kbps, 48kHz

**App Store Preview**:
- Resolution: 1920x1080
- Codec: H.264 or HEVC
- Bitrate: 5-8 Mbps
- Frame rate: 30fps
- Duration: 15-30 seconds

**Social Media**:
- Resolution: 1080x1920 (9:16 vertical)
- Codec: H.264
- Bitrate: 4-6 Mbps
- Duration: 15 seconds
